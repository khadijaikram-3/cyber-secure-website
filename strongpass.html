Expert Password Tips

Tip 1

Use the "Three Random Words" method: combine three unrelated words like "Horse$Battery$Staple" for memorable yet secure passwords.

Tip 2

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible. Even if your password is compromised, 2FA provides an additional security layer.

Tip 3

Change passwords immediately if a service reports a data breach. Monitor haveibeenpwned.com to check if your accounts have been compromised.

Tip 4

Never share passwords via email, text, or phone calls. Legitimate companies will never ask for your password through these channels.
